**__List of commands (Quotation marks should be included):__**

**!join** - Lets me join the voice channel
**!leave** - Lets me leave the voice channel

**__These only work when I'm in a voice channel:__**

**!play "song name"** - Lets me play a song (only works when I'm in a voice channel)
**!queue "song name"** - Adds a song to the queue 
**!pause** - Pauses the current song
**!resume** - Resumes the paused song
**!skip** - Skips song to the next one in your queue
